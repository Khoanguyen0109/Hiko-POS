# Hiko POS — Business Features

**Last updated:** May 20, 2026

A restaurant point-of-sale and operations platform for taking orders, managing staff, tracking inventory, and running the business day to day.

---

## Point of Sale

- Take orders from a menu organized by category
- Add toppings and customizations to dishes
- Build a cart, apply discounts, and place orders
- Look up customers at checkout
- Apply coupon codes and loyalty rewards
- Generate bills and print receipts
- Support dine-in (table-based) and delivery orders

---

## Orders

- View and manage all orders in one place
- Track order status: pending → in progress → ready → completed
- Edit items on an existing order
- Attach a customer and promotions after the order is created
- Support delivery platforms: Shopee, Grab, BeFood, XanhSM
- Full order history and change log

---

## Payments

- Accept Cash, Banking, and Card
- Track payment status per order
- View daily cash vs banking totals
- End-of-shift checkout: staff enter counted cash and banking; system compares to completed orders in the scheduled shift window
- Admin (and managers) can view all store shifts for a date and check out on behalf of any assigned member
- Mismatch warnings with required notes; managers review all checkouts per day

---

## Tables

- Manage restaurant tables (seats, status)
- Mark tables as available or booked

---

## Menu

- Manage dishes (name, price, image, availability)
- Organize dishes into categories
- Manage toppings/add-ons (e.g. Kem, Matcha)
- Turn items on/off without deleting them

---

## Promotions & Discounts

- Create promotions: percentage off, fixed amount off, happy hour pricing
- Target whole orders, specific dishes, or categories
- Set coupon codes with usage limits
- Schedule happy hour by day and time
- View promotion performance

---

## Customers & Loyalty

- Maintain a customer database (name, phone, etc.)
- Search customers at the POS
- View customer order history
- Run reward programs (e.g. free dish or discount after N visits/spend)
- Redeem rewards at checkout

---

## Daily Overview (Home)

- Today's revenue and order counts
- Cash vs banking breakdown
- Revenue by delivery platform
- Recent orders at a glance
- Low stock alerts

---

## Analytics Dashboard

- Revenue trends and breakdowns
- Admin: view and filter shift checkouts by date range, status (balanced/mismatch), and member; delete a checkout so the member can submit again
- Top-selling dishes and categories
- Payment method and vendor performance
- Promotion, salary, storage, and loyalty metrics
- Filter by date range (today, week, month, custom)

---

## Inventory & Storage

- Track stock items and minimum levels
- Record stock imports (receiving from suppliers)
- Record stock exports (usage/consumption)
- Manage suppliers
- Low stock warnings on the home screen

---

## Expenses

- Record business expenses (rent, utilities, supplies, etc.)
- Categorize expenses and assign vendors
- Track paid vs unpaid expenses
- Expense reports by category, vendor, and month

---

## Staff & Scheduling

- Create reusable shift templates
- Build weekly schedules and assign staff to shifts
- Shift checkout at end of assigned shift (cash/banking reconciliation vs POS totals)
- Views: by store, by member, or personal schedule
- Detect scheduling conflicts
- Log and approve extra/overtime hours
- Mark extra work as paid

---

## Payroll

- Calculate monthly salary from scheduled shifts and extra work
- Staff can view their own salary breakdown
- Admin overview of all staff payroll

---

## Employee Recognition (Tickets)

- Managers award recognition tickets to staff
- Monthly leaderboard
- Staff can view their own tickets

---

## Multi-Store & Team

- Operate multiple store locations
- Staff pick which store they're working at
- Per-store roles: Owner, Manager, Staff
- Global admin manages stores, members, and store assignments

---

## User Accounts

- Secure login for all staff
- Roles: Admin, Waiter, Cashier, User
- Profile and password management
- Personal view of salary and tickets
